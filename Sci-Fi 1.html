Spacetime issues

First, let me explain spacetime versus space and/or time. Spacetime refers to space, objects, energy, mass, etc. combined with time in a 4D measurement system. Spacetime measurements are measurements that include location, distance, velocity, etc combined with the specific time of measurement. They are therefore more specific than individual space and/or time measurements.
